UGA-MPAS Viewer

I've left the nest, spread my wings, and I'm free for all to see!
00z run goes out to 48hr, 12/18z go out 24hr. there is no 06z run. in addition, unfinished runs are displayed.
if you see a blank image in any hour before 24 (48 for 00z runs), it means the run has not finished yet (aka in progress).
